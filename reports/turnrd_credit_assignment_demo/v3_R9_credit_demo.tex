\noindent{\small\textit{Greedy K=1 rollouts on AlfWorld held-out tasks. Each turn shows the observation on the first line and the agent's action on the second; only the action is shaded by per-turn credit (green = positive, red = negative) because the action is what receives reward / blame. Color saturation is normalized per trajectory. The most-decisive turn in successes (largest positive credit) and the most-fatal turn in failures (largest negative credit) are marked with $\bigstar$. Turn 0 is the initial observation (uncolored, no credit). By construction, per-turn credits sum to the trajectory's final reward $R$ via the v-projection.}}
